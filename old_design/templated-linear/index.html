Insight Marine Survey and Services

Welcome to Insight Marine Survey and Services

We provide services throughout a vessels lifecyle from pre-purchase to end of operation.

We also provide access to a wealth of extra technical expertise including financial modelling, forensic investigation and simulation and modelling services.

General Cargo Discharge Surveys

We conduct discharge monitoring and surveys to ensure safe unloading of cargo. The entire operation is methodically supervised and documented and your interests will be addressed in the best manner.

Food, Agriculture and Other Consumer Products

Each type of cargo has to be handled in a specific way and the closed nature of containers can conceal possible damage until arrival at the final destination. We provide advice and guidance for all types of cargo handling.

Vessel Condition Surveys

Through vessel condition surveying our experts can determine the condition of any type of vessel whether it is sea going or not.

Insurance Surveys

We provide thorough inspection of the vessel’s structural and watertight integrity, system’s installations, and operating condition, including performance and issue a comprehensive report.
